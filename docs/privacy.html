Privacy Policy

Seisuitei · Koi Pond

This Privacy Policy describes how the Seisuitei mobile application (the “App”) handles information. It applies to the version of the App distributed through the Apple App Store and TestFlight.

The short version

The App does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. It contains no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs that talk to a server. Everything you do in the pond stays on your device.

Information we do not collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, address, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • We do not collect device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV) for tracking purposes.
  • We do not collect crash logs, usage analytics, or telemetry of any kind.
  • We do not require an account, and we do not authenticate you against any service.
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, contacts, calendar, photos, location, health data, or HomeKit.

Information stored on your device

The App saves your progress — the koi you have raised, varieties you have discovered, food given, and similar gameplay state — to your device’s local storage (browser localStorage inside the embedded WKWebView). This data never leaves your device. You can erase it at any time by deleting the App.

Network activity

The App does not require an internet connection. It does not make outbound network requests. If you launch it offline, every feature works identically.

Children’s privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Apple’s policies for apps directed to children. Because the App collects no information at all, it collects none from children either.

Third-party services

The App is built with the open-source Capacitor framework. Capacitor itself does not transmit data on the App’s behalf. The App bundles no third-party analytics, advertising, attribution, or social SDKs.

Your rights

Because the App collects no personal data, there is no data for us to access, correct, export, or delete on your behalf. Removing the App from your device removes all locally stored state.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL with a new effective date below. Material changes will be communicated through the App Store update notes.
